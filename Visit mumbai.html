Mumbai

Mumbai is a coastal city located along the west coast of India. It is famous for film production and is called the commercial capital. People worldwide come to Mumbai to work in the film industry and to get fame. There is no shortage of electricity and basic amenities, and the city's water supply is excellent.

Mumbai is Famous For: Monuments & Iconic structures Gateway of India. The Gateway sees hundreds of visitors a day. ... Taj Mahal Hotel. The Taj Mahal Palace is one of the most iconic hotels in India. ... Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus (CST) You can take stunning pictures from the viewing balcony opposite CST.
