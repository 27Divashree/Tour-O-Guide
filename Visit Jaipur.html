Jaipur

The capital and largest city of the Indian state of Rajasthan is Jaipur. Because of the predominately pink colour scheme of its buildings, Jaipur is sometimes referred to as the "Pink City." The city of Jaipur was established in 1727 by Jai Singh II, the ruler of Amer and a Kachhwaha Rajput

Jaipur is filled with attractive monuments, royal palaces, alluring cultures, and so on. The city's architecture was greatly impacted by the Mughal architectural renaissance that occurred in Northern India in the 17th century. As a result, a lot of it has an architectural style that is typical of the Muslim world.Jaipur is widely known for being a tourist destination due to its beautiful features and attractive architectures

Bit more on Jaipur

The Pink City of India is renowned for its unique characteristics and urban fabric, which cannot be found anywhere else. Jaipur is a photographer's paradise because of the striking contrasts between the shadows on the buildings and their pink hues, as well as the buildings' intricate niches of decoration . The streets of Jaipur provide something for everyone with their variety of colour tones, lively musical background, mouthwatering cuisine, and numerous bazaars.
